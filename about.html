About Us

Black Students in Commercial Real Estate

BSCRE is a student-led organization at The Ohio State University focused on helping Black students explore and launch careers in commercial real estate.

We exist to close the representation gap in commercial real estate by creating a space where Black students can learn the fundamentals, build a network, and get real exposure to deals, firms, and career paths.

Our Focus

  • Development & acquisitions
  • Real estate private equity & finance
  • Brokerage, asset management, and more

What Members Can Expect

  • Educational sessions on underwriting and deal analysis
  • Guest speakers and alumni panels
  • Networking with industry professionals and student orgs
  • Support with internships, case competitions, and recruiting
